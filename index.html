Manifesto

We're Not Building Another Battlefy

The competitive gaming space is flooded with platforms that optimize for volume — endless low-stakes brackets, ghost entries, and forgettable matches. LastRound exists because competition should feel rare, valuable, and real.

Every tournament is designed with intentionality. Credits ensure commitment. Limited entries create tension. Structured formats build narratives worth watching and playing.

We focus on underserved competitive games — the fighters, tactical shooters, and legacy titles that have passionate communities but no platform that takes them seriously.

Your PnL is public. Your trophies are earned. Your credits unlock the biggest stages. We're not trying to be the biggest — we're building the one that matters.

Principles

Skin in the Game

Credit buy-ins filter out casual entries. Every match has real stakes, real consequences.

Scarcity Over Scale

Fewer events, bigger payoffs. Every tournament feels like an occasion, not content churn.

Transparency First

Public PnL, verified results, dispute resolution. Trust is the product.

Earn Your Stage

BANGER events aren't bought — they're earned through credits. The best players get the biggest stages.

Underserved First

CoD, Rocket League, Gears of War, fighters — communities with passion but no real platform.
